<h2>HOW TO PLAY</h2>

You play as a ghost running away from Pacman and collecting ghosts

Use buttons a,s,d,w or navigation buttons to move

When you eat the power pill, you will have 7 seconds to become invincible and can attack the Pac Man. When the Power Pill time runs out, the Pac Man will revive randomly.

When all the ghosts or pacman are eaten, the player wins

When you eat a ghost, you will get +1 point. When you eat a pacman, you will get +50 points.
